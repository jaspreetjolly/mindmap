# Office 365 E1 Plus
## Productivity & Collaboration
   - Web-based Office Apps (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
   - Exchange Online (50GB mailbox)
   - OneDrive for Business (1TB)
   - SharePoint Online (1TB + 1GBpU)
   - Microsoft Teams
   - Viva (Engage, Connections, Learning & Insights)
   - Streams, Planner, To-do, Forms, Lists

## Security & Compliance
   - Data Loss Prevention (DLP) for Office workloads (Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive)
   - Anti-Malware and Anti-Spam protection
   - Manual Retention Labels (Exchange, SharePoint, Teams)
   - Basic eDiscovery (content search only)

## Identity & Device Management
- Microsoft Intune
  - MDM (BYOD, Company-owned, Kiosk)
  - MAM (App-level control)
  - Compliance Policies
  - App Protection Policies
  - Device Configuration Profiles
  - Patch Management
    

- Entra ID Plan 1
    - Identity Protection (MFA & SSO)
    - Conditional Access
    - Self-service Password Reset (Hybrid) 
    - Group-based Access Management
